What is

Name This Chord is a humble hobby project. I`m not a professional musician, even an experienced hobbyst. I just enjoy studying music theory and also programming. Here you can draw some guitar chords, find intervals and study chord formation. Hope it helps!

Chords & Intervals

Chords are simply varying combinations of intervals. Most basic chords are built using three notes. Chords are built on their root note (the starting note) followed by other notes. The other notes gives the chord a "feel" and can make it sound good or bad in a given context (harmony).
